Study
UNIQUENESS IS THE DETAIL
LNT Design & Construction uses a tailor-made approach for each client, full of intellectual rigor and creativity, to create stimulating places with a strong visual impact. We design unique schemes that involve everything from structural work to furnishings, with great attention to detail. The firm refines each tailor-made project, respecting the times and the budget. The experience in the sector and our resources allow us to make the whole process efficient and pleasant. We constantly monitor each project to ensure the work meets our standards of perfection.
Services
INTERIOR DESIGN
Overcoming the concept of simple architecture and in line with new market demands, we design and renovate interiors by concentrating emotions, comfort and ergonomic needs.
HOME STYLING
A step towards uniqueness. What style do you have in mind for your rooms? We design spaces for individual, creative well-being and always with great attention to that little bit more.
FURNITURE DESIGN
To facilitate and improve all the user's activities, we design custom-made ergonomic furniture that facilitate and offer maximum comfort in everyday life.
PURCHASE MEDIATION
The close collaboration with important brands allows us to carry out a targeted search, on behalf of the customer, of furniture, materials and furnishings more akin to their needs.
